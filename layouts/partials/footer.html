about

Maddy Bruster is a graphic designer and writer living and working in New York City. She has worked as an art director, graphic designer, front-end developer and copywriter with organizations of various sizes.

From 2020-2022 she held design and web development roles at Partner & Partners. Since 2020 she has run an evolving independent design practice, IT HAS TO BE REAL, operating as an LLC. Working as IT HAS TO BE REAL, Maddy collaborates with design and architecture studios, small businesses, startups, cultural institutions, artists and individuals to realize ideas across printed and digital matter.

She has written for AIGA's Eye on Design, The Creative Independent, Dimepiece, New York Review of Architecture and others.

In the capacity of IT HAS TO BE REAL she works on projects that range from the hyper-commercial (websites for software companies) to one-off articles and illustrations driven by her own interests. In an era of increasing specialization she remains a committed generalist and jack-of-all-trades. Her clients come from industries as various as art, architecture, technology, government, advocacy, politics and sports. Please get in touch below.
