<p>Can you imagine coming to the new world and being so homesick or tired or totally unimaginative that after traveling halfway around the globe on a boat and riding in a covered wagon to pound nails into wood and lay the floorboards of a house in a town that doesn’t exist yet, naming that town after the place you started from?</p>
