Maddy Bruster is a writer and graphic designer living in New York City. She was born in 1997 in Austin, Texas.

Since 2020 she has run an independent design practice, collaborating with design and architecture studios, small businesses, startups, cultural institutions, and individuals to realize ideas across printed and digital matter. She has also worked as a front-end developer, a copywriter, and as a graphic designer with organizations of various sizes.

She has contributed to

New York Review of Architecture
AIGA's Eye on Design
