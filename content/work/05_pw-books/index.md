Series design for [Phenomenal World Books](https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/series/PHWB.html) from the Univeristy of Chicago Press and JFI's Phenomenal World. The series design leverages a gradation from off-white to off-black in Phenomenal World's red + blue hues, generating a potential multiplicity of covers. Photos for each cover were cropped into the Phenomenal World diamond, and titles flow around key parts of the images.
